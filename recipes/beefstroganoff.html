Beef Stroganoff

Description

Seared beef in a sour cream mushroom gravy. Time-tested comfort dish for fall or winter and reheats well.

Ingredients

  • 600 g / 1.2 lb scotch fillet steak or boneless ribeye
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 300 g / 10 oz mushroom
  • 40 g / 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 cups / 500 ml beef broth
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 150 ml / 2/3 cup sour cream
  • Salt and pepper
  • 8-10 oz pasta or egg noodles of choice
  • Chopped chives

Steps

  1. Flatten steaks about 1/3" thick, slice into 1/5" strips discarding excess fat
  2. Sprinkle with pinch of salt and pepper
  3. Heat 1 tbsp oil in large skillet over high heat. Scatter half beef in skillet. Quickly spread with tongs. Leave untouched for 30 seconds until browned. Turn beef quickly and leave untouched for 30 seconds to brown. Remove onto plate
  4. Add remaining 1 tbsp oil and repeat with remaining beef
  5. Turn heat to medium high, add butter, melt, add onion cook for 1 minute, add mushrooms
  6. Cook mushrooms until gold
  7. Add flour, cook, stirring for 1 minute
  8. Add half broth while stirring, once incorporated add remaining broth
  9. Stir then add sour cream and mustard. Stir until all is incorporated
  10. Bring to simmer, reduce heat to medium low. Once it thickens to consistency of pouring cream (3-5 minutes) adjust salt and pepper to taste
  11. Add beef back in (including plate juices). Simmer for 1 minute, then remove from stove
  12. Serve over pasta or egg noodles sprinkled with chives
