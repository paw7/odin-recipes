Waldorf Red Cake

Description

A rich chocolate cake. In the same family as other red velvet cakes with decadent frosting and moist cake layer.

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup shortening
  • 2 oz. red food coloring
  • 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 heaping tablespoons cocoa
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar

Steps

  1. Cream shortening, sugar and eggs
  2. Make a paste of food coloring and cocoa
  3. Add ingredients to creamed mixture as well as buttermilk and sifted flour, soda, salt combination alternately, starting and ending with dry ingredients.
  4. Add vanilla and vinegar.
  5. Bake 20-30 minutes in 3 8" round pans, 350 degrees
  6. Cool in pans few minutes then cool on racks
  7. Frost with powdered sugar frosting or cooked egg white frosting
